Tutorial

Welcome to Extracto, your go-to tool for effortless data extraction and customization. Follow these simple steps to make the most of our features :

Step 1 : Enter URL or Open CSV File

  1. Enter ABS-CBN URL :

    • Paste the link of the ABS-CBN News article you want to extract data from.
    • Example : 'https://www.abs-cbn.com/news/article123

  2. Open CSV File :

    • Alternatively, upload an existing CSV file to extract data.
    • Note : Ensure the first column of the CSV file contains URLs.

Step 2 : Extract Data

Click on the "Extract Data" button, and our web scraping tool will efficiently analyze the webpage content, extracting relevant text.

Step 3 : Customize Results

  1. Find Words :

    • Search for specific words in the extracted data.
    • Example : "Coronavirus, Technology, Environment

  2. Substitute Words :

    • Replace certain words throughout the data.
    • Example : (Mother : Father), (Cat Dog)

Step 4 : Download Results.

Click on the "Download CSV" button to save the extracted and customized data as a .csv file.

Step 5 : Additional Options

  • Copy to Clipboard : Copy the extracted data for quick use
  • Data Privacy : Your information is secure and will never be sold or shared. Privacy Policy

That's it! Explore the power of Extracto in simplifying your data extraction and customization needs.
